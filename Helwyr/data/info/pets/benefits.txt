[Legendary Pets Overall Benefits]
- 5% experience boost per level.
- Passively a charm collector.
- Now sends you home and heals you for 10% of your maximum health
when you are below 20% of your maximum health.
- Automatically collects coins from killed monsters, and puts into
inventory/money pouch.
- Now automatically adds items into your inventory from monster drops
if the value is greater than your lootbeam threshold.
[Using an item on legendary pet]
 *Alching*
- When alching items from your pets abilities, he will note noted/non noted items.
if you alch noted items in your inventory, he will alch the entire amount of the noted items.
 *Noting*
- When using the note item ability, he notes all of the items in your inventory, instead of just the one item.
